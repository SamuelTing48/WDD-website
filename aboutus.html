About Us

Live, Learn, and Have Fun at APU Accommodation



Living away from home is a big step and the start of a new chapter. Our contemporary accommodation options are designed with you in mind, to provide a secure and welcoming home for you to create memories that will last a lifetime.

Our APU On-Campus Residence and Satellite Campus Residence @ TPM at APU provide students the university life beyond classrooms. We provide everything that you need from common pantry to residence’s lounge areas. As a resident of APU, you'll be living in an environment designed not only to house students but also to foster community and intellectual growth.

Get in touch with our Accommodation Team

Have a question to ask? We are always ready to address your questions and concerns about our accommodation.

Speak to our Accommodation Team on campus at Level 3 or email rsvp@apu.edu.my for more information on available types of accommodation, facilities, and price.

You may also download our Accommodation Booking Form and send it to us via email. All room sizes are available on a first-come, first-served basis.
