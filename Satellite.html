Satellite Campus Residence is within Technology Park Malaysia (TPM) and next to the APU Campus. It has triple sharing and single occupancy options. Some of the rooms are equipped with Air Conditioning. Each floor has shared common bathrooms, equipped with water heaters. A Common Kitchen is available at each floor and is equipped with stove, microwave oven and refrigerator. There is also a shared Study Area with Wi-Fi Internet in the Study Lounge. The rental is inclusive of utilities – water & electricity. Air Conditioners can only be operated from 9 pm to 8 am. Self Service Laundry Facilities are available on ‘Pay as you use’ basis.

There is one type of accommodation, with different room options within:
  • Standard Room
