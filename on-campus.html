APU On-Campus Residence is located within APU’s Campus in Technology Park Malaysia. The APU On-Campus Residence is equipped with 24/7 security and access card control. Students staying in the APU Residence will have access to the campus facilities directly, such as the SweatZone, Swimming Pool, Residence Lounge and more.
There are four types of accommodation, with different room options within:
  • Premium
  • Premium++
  • Dual Key Premium
  • Apartment

Students with Special Needs

We welcome our students with disabilities or medical and other support needs. The Disability Team can support you to access appropriate on-campus accommodation including access to an en-suite room, with a room with an accessible kitchen. All our lifts are disable friendly. A fridge is provided in most rooms to store medication or for other disability related purpose.

We have an On-Campus clinic and 24 hours paramedics and welfare support for medical emergency.

Welfare and Disability Support Office
Level 3, Spine, Student Services - APU
Email - welfare@apu.edu.my
